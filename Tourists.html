Tourist Places in Cibubur

Even though Cibubur is a confusing and “identity-crisis” area, we have 3 main tourist attractions here. The first and the oldest one is called Buperta Cibubur. Buperta stands for Bumi Perkemahan dan Graha Wisata Wiladatika. This place is often used as a venue for scout (Pramuka) events. There are also many outdoor activities here such as paintball and outbound. The second and the newest one is TransStudioCibubur theme park. It's the second biggest theme park of Trans Studio aside from TSM Bandung. Last but not least, we have Taman Buah Mekarsari which located on the border of Cibubur and Cileungsi

01

Buperta Cibubur

If you are wondering about a place to camp or hosting a scout event, Buperta Cibubur is a perfect place for it. This place has a huge green-area to do fun outdoor activities with your friends or family.

02

Trans Studio Cibubur

This place recently went viral in TikTok. TSM Cibubur is an indoor theme park with a technology-space themed. They have various rides and live performances that can entertain the whole family.

03

Taman Buah Mekarsari

This place is classified as one of the oldest tourist attractions in Cibubur. Mekarsari is a biodiversity conservation center for tropical fruits from all around Indonesia and the world. But unfortunately, they are not open for public every day
