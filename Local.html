Local Food

There is no specific “local food” from Cibubur. But we do have a Jakarta local food named Kerak Telor. Kerak Telor has been one of my favorite comfort foods since I was like 3 years old. It was made with the ingredients of white sticky rice, chicken or duck eggs, marinated dried shrimp plus fried shallots, then added with ground spices in the form of roasted coconut, red chilies, kencur, ginger, granulated pepper, salt and sugar sand.

In Cibubur, there is this one Japanese restaurant that is quite famous and well-known for its sushi named Umaku. I might consider this as “Cibubur-Local-Food '' since we don't have any specific local food. Lots of people from outside Cibubur are dying to try Umaku.
